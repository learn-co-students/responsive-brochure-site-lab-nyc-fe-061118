Breakout Star

This year's breakout star will be Jerick McKinnon!

An elusive back that has spent much of his time sharing the carries the past several years.

Now with his own backfield in San Francisco, he should blossom into a top ten running back.

Biggest Bust

This year's biggest bust will be Kareem Hunt.

Hunt was a rookie sensation last year for the Kansas City Chiefs and finished as a top 5 RB.

But with a new offensive coordinator and Spencer Ware returning, Hunt will have a much harder time getting as many opportunities as he did in 2017.

Deepest Sleeper

This year's biggest sleeper is Mike Williams!

Williams had high expectations as the Charger's 1st round pick in 2017, but injuries sidelined him for most of the season.

Now healthy, look for Williams to become a main player in the Charger's offense, especially with the injury to Hunter Henry.
